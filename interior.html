Interior Design

Whether you are looking for simple wall paintings or want an entire interior makeover, Unispace is an innovative team of passionate personnel who enjoy the art of transformation and rebirth of your work-space helping you achieve any design that impeccably matches your requirements!

Our main goal is to make your home look good and feel great. We do this by offering extensive superb construction services and improvised designed homes through our construction, interior and exterior designers that are guaranteed to fit your taste, budget and lifestyle. We have access to over 100,000 items from all around the world. Our team of experienced designers will help you select the perfect room in your home.

Based on your theme, we are inclined to offer advice where necessary contingent to the available trends and elegance in the current market, and ingeniously mixing up various styles to guarantee an authentic aftermath of your preferred design and decoration. We have substantial knowledge on numerous polishes and colors that could be used to give your home a fresh look. In 2018 we revolutionized high-end home decor by combining a first class interior design service with our exclusive luxury furniture collection bringing a timeless new aesthetic to design conscious home owners in Kenya. We create a variety of styles for all sorts of spaces, including: 

  • Kitchen & Bathroom Design -

    We design kitchens and bathrooms from scratch using the latest trends and styles available in the market today. Our designs include everything from custom cabinetry, countertops, flooring, lighting, plumbing fixtures and more.

  • Home Office Design -

    Our experienced designers will help you create a beautiful work environment at home so you could get more done while enjoying your family time at the same time.

  • Floor Plans/3D Rendering -

    We will create 3D renderings of your floor plan based on your specific needs so you could see what it would precisely look like before the construction commencement. This also grants you an idea of what kind of changes you might need to make to avoid unnecessary surprises when it's finally time to move into your new home.

  • Custom close Design -

    • Custom closets give your closet space style while maximizing. Our award-winning combination is available for all projects of any size from single room, feature-wall or storage solution to the furnishing of complete properties from shop fitting to work space also from 1-bedroomed apartments to 5-bedroomed houses.
