ABOUT

Unispace is a company that deals with interior design and renovation. The company has been around from 2018 and growing ever since. It offers services such as; Interior design, Decoration and Renovation Services, Furniture Manufacturing and Commercial shop fitting. Our team is comprised of talented designers who love what they do! They bring out the best in every client by carefully listening to their needs and helping them achieve their vision through thoughtful planning and execution. We value our clients as we focus on excellent health and safety record incorporated in our true service culture and willingness to listen, as well as to our flexible and pragmatic attitude to design and delivery. You will find us to be experts in our profession and to be honest, personable and straight-dealing as people. We also are great believers in the transformative power of creative collaboration.

To create environments that deliver on every level; aesthetic, practical, commercial, experiential, by applying intelligence to design through developing personal vision for every project, setting the most uncompromising standards and working side by side with our clients to bring that vision to life and becoming world-class designers and contractors of luxury hotels, resorts and private residences.
